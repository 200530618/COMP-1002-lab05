Biography

I was born and raised in India. I spent my most of the time in my hometown. I learned many things and skills there. My hometown is Panipat, which is a small town in Haryana provence. But Panipat is called the city of weavers, as it produces textiles and carpets. It is the biggest center for quality blankets and carpets in India and has a hand loom weaving industry. Panipat city is the biggest centre of “shoddy yarn” in the World. I am proud of my city.

I passed my senior secondary from Duhan Public School (Rohtak, Haryana). After that I decided to complete my higher education. So I came to Canada. Now I'm an International student at Georgian College in Barrie, Ontario. My course name is Computer Programming which duration is two years programme.
